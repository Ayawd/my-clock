The Coldplay

ABOUT

Coldplay are a British rock band formed in London in 1996. They consist of vocalist and pianist Chris Martin, guitarist Jonny Buckland, bassist Guy Berryman, drummer Will Champion and creative director Phil Harvey.[3] They met at University College London and began playing music together from 1996 to 1998, first calling themselves Pectoralz and then Starfish.

On 26 September 2019, Global Citizen announced that Coldplay would perform at Global Goal Live: The Possible Dream on 26 September 2020.[185] On 18 October 2019, mysterious black-and-white posters began appearing in various countries around the world, with the band in vintage-style clothing and a date showing 22 November 1919. The band also changed their profile pictures on social media to a sun and moon, making fans speculate an imminent release of new material.[186] On 19 October 2019, a cryptic 5 second teaser was released on social media with orchestral music in the background.[187] On 21 October 2019, in a letter sent to fans, the band announced that their eighth studio album would be titled Everyday Life[188] and that it would be a double album, with the first half titled Sunrise and the second half titled Sunset.
